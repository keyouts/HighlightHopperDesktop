# Privacy Policy

## What We Store

Highlight Hopper may store the following information:

- Highlighted text and the color you chose for it
- Optional notes you attach to individual highlights
- Metadata used to reapply highlights (such as timestamp, source page, and grouping key)
- The canonical URL used to group highlights (for example, the page URL or a hyperlink target URL)

---

## Where Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device:

### Browser Extension
- `browser.storage.local`

### Desktop Version
- Local application storage on your machine

Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

---

## Sharing and Transmission

Highlight Hopper does **not**:

- Send highlights or notes to remote servers
- Share data with third parties
- Use analytics or tracking services

Your content remains private and local.

---

## How to Delete Your Data

You can remove data in the following ways:

- Delete individual highlights inside the app
- Export and manually edit your CSV
- Clear the extension’s storage through your browser settings
- Uninstall the extension or desktop application
- Delete the application’s local data folder

---

## Permissions

The application may request the following permissions depending on the version.

### Browser Extension

- **storage** — Save highlights and notes locally
- **activeTab** — Interact with the page where you create highlights
- **downloads** — Export highlights and notes to `.txt` or `.csv` files

### Desktop App

The desktop version only uses local file and application storage necessary for saving your highlights and exported files.
