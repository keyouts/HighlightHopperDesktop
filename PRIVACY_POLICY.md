**What We Store**



**Highlight Hopper may store the following information:**



&#x20;   Highlighted text and the color you chose for it



&#x20;   Optional notes you attach to individual highlights



&#x20;   Metadata used to reapply highlights (timestamp, source page, grouping key)



&#x20;   The canonical URL used to group highlights (for example, the page URL or a hyperlink target URL)



**Where Data Is Stored**



**All data is stored locally on your device:**



&#x20;   In the browser extension version: browser.storage.local



&#x20;   In the desktop version: local application storage on your machine



Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

Sharing and Transmission



***Highlight Hopper does not:***



&#x20;   ***send highlights or notes to remote servers***



&#x20;   ***share data with third parties***



&#x20;   ***use analytics or tracking services***



Your content remains private and local.



**How to Delete Your Data**



You can remove data in the following ways:



&#x20;   Delete individual highlights inside the app



&#x20;   Export and manually edit your CSV



&#x20;   Clear the extension’s storage through your browser settings



&#x20;   Uninstall the extension or desktop application



&#x20;   Delete the application’s local data folder



**Permissions**



The application may request the following permissions depending on the version:

Browser Extension



&#x20;   **storage — to save highlights and notes locally**



&#x20;   **activeTab — to interact with the page where you create highlights**



&#x20;   **downloads — to export highlights and notes to .txt or .csv files**



**Desktop App**



&#x20;   **File access — to import and export CSV files**



&#x20;   **Local storage — to save highlights and notes on your device**
